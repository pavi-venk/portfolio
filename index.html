Hello, I’m Pavi!

I’m a mechanical engineering student focused on robotics, autonomy and systems-level problem solving. 

I’m a third-year undergraduate at Northwestern University with a robotics concentration, pursuing an AI minor and the Segal Design Certificate. My work spans hands-on engineering and research, from designing and launching rockets with NUSTARS to exploring swarm robotics in the Center of Robotics and Biosystems. I’m driven by curiosity, learning and building systems that work. Through leadership roles in the Society of Women Engineers and collaborative team projects, I enjoy bringing people together to turn complex ideas into real ones.

When I’m not building robots or rockets, I spend time with friends and family or travel somewhere new! Having grown up in India, China, Japan and Switzerland, I adapt quickly to new places. I love engaging with diverse perspectives and drawing inspiration from the people I meet.
